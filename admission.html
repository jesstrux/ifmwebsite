Code of Conduct

We expect the highest level of discipline, friendliness, team work, tolerance and good citizenship from all our students and faculty. This all begins with proper dress code without which you won't be allowed past the IFM gates even in case of emergencies so please beware. Hate speech, discrimination of any kind, loitering, sexual misconduct and promiscous behavior are strictly prohibited and may result in imidiate termination without refund or prior notice.

Certificates Scrutiny

To avoid unnecessary troubles, please make sure you bring all your required certificates as indicatedin your joining instructions. Beware that they will be placed under intense scrutiny so please make sure they are valid. Fake tendered certificates may also be reported to law enforcement organisation and may result in jail time.

Fee Beneficiariees

IFM is not affiliated with any fee beneficiariees and will therefore not be responsible to make sure all your affairs are in order. Please make sure you have cleared any queries with your sponsors before you come to the university because any different situation wont be excused by any of our support staff.
