About IFM

The Institute Of Finance Management is the leading financial training pioneer in the United Republic of Tanzania. We stay on top not because we were first in but because we continuously find ways to stay relevant, arming our students with the latest knowledge to make them industry leads when they go out into the wide unpredictable world of finance and technology.

Since the late 90s(1997 to be exact), we have taken pride in nurturing the next wave of finance, banking and tax experts and since 2007 we added Techonology expertise training (Computer Science and Information Techonology) to the mix after realising that the future is technology supporting from the basics to the very expert of decision support systems in both the finance sector and other related and supporting sectors as well such as agriculture and fishing.

A word from the Rector

Ours is a cohort system between the management, faculty members, students and the many financial and technology institution countrywide making great on our promise of giving the best of the best back to the society in building a well informed generation of accountable and properly trained finance and technology individuals. Joining us means that you're not only going to get access to lifetime like thinking friends but also become a member of our large extended family through our alumni and partner programs and like all families, we do know how to take care of our own during as well as post your academic period to make sure you don't lose your way.

I really hope you will find your way to us, and we're exitedly looking forward to seeing you right here at IFM when you come either to visit and get the feel of the surrounding or to become a permanent member of the family either as a student, faculty member or both.
