A word from the Rector

The Institute Of Finance Management is the leading financial training pioneer in the United Republic of Tanzania.
Since the late 90s(1997 to be exact), we have taken pride in nurturing the next wave of finance, banking and tax experts and since 2007 we added Techonology expertise training (Computer Science and Information Techonology) to the mix.
Ours is a cohort system between the management, faculty members, students and the many financial and technology institution countrywide making great on our promise of giving the best of the best back to the society in building a well informed generation of accountable and properly trained finance and technology individuals. Joining us means that you're not only going to get access to lifetime like thinking friends but also become a member of our large extended family through our alumni and partner programs and like all families, we do know how to take care of our own during as well as post your academic period. We hope you find your way to us, and looking forward to seeing you soon.
